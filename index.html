Methodology based on Mike Burrell's blog post.

To expand on the idea, I will be calculating the average "mass arrival" dates for each species as they change year to year, and how accurate each year turned out to be. This should highlight the effects of climate change: showing exactly how each species' migration timing has altered. I also plan to include fall migration in the future.

All bird data is from eBird.

eBird. 2021. eBird: An online database of bird distribution and abundance [web application]. eBird, Cornell Lab of Ornithology, Ithaca, New York. Available: http://www.ebird.org. (Accessed: April 5, 2022).
